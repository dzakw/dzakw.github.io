Section

Section is a container for grouping related content.

Example

Grouping related content in a section:

London

London is the capital city of England. It is the most populous city in the United Kingdom, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. It is often called the City of Light. Paris is the home of the most visited art museum in the world, the Louvre, as well as the Eiffel Tower.
